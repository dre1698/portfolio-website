Fanclub Branding

Tools: Affinity Designer, Affinity Photo

This branding project was created for a local football fanclub based in Bochum. The goal was to design a bold and simple identity that fans could use both digitally and in real life.

Design Focus:
The circular logo was used across WhatsApp, shirts, and stickers. It features the silhouette of a fan standing in their usual spot inside the stadium – representing everyone in the group. The club’s home and away colors were used to ensure connection and recognizability.

Scarf Design:
A matching scarf was created to capture the energy and unity of matchdays. It includes elements from the logo, strong typography, and a color scheme that fans can wear with pride.
