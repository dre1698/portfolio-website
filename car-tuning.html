Car Tuning Through Time

Tools: Affinity Designer, After Effects, iMovie

This 2D animated video explores the visual and cultural evolution of car tuning from past to present. The short animation presents a timeline of design trends, modifications, and tuning styles across several decades.

Visual Concept:
Each decade is represented through stylized illustrations, color palettes, and motion design that reflect the aesthetic of the time. Animated typography and transitions help guide the viewer through the story.

Creative Focus:
The goal was to combine visual storytelling with educational content in a fast-paced and engaging format, designed for platforms like YouTube or Instagram Reels.

Outcome:
The project strengthened my skills in illustration, animation timing, and visual rhythm. It also taught me how to simplify complex information for visual formats.
