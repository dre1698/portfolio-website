Robot Dreams

Tools: Unity, C#, Autodesk Maya

In this 3D Unity game, you play as a vacuum robot with dreams of greatness. The game takes place in a fully navigable apartment where your goal is to clean up dust while managing your battery life and strategic movements.

Gameplay Mechanics:

  • Battery-based movement system with a real-time recharge zone
  • Collectible upgrades downloaded from the Wi-Fi router
  • Time pressure mechanics and a “clean-to-complete” objective
  • Custom 3D assets and environment layout

Design Focus:
The game emphasizes smart movement choices, resource awareness, and a whimsical personality. All 3D elements were modeled in Maya and integrated into Unity with custom scripts and animations.

Outcome:
This project combined game logic, level design, 3D modeling, and player feedback loops to create an engaging gameplay experience.
